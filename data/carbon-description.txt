<ul>
  <li><b>Advanced moderation tools:</b><ul>
    <li>Mute people or channels.</li>
    <li>Kick anyone out</li>
    <li>Ban people for specific amount of time, regardless of whether they are on the server or not!</li>
    <li>Keep track of their usernames and nicknames, when did they create an account and join.</li>
    <li>Add notes about people, keep track of all their previous kicks or bans.</li>
	<li>You can also nuke the whole channel (wipe all the messages) or clear only specific amount of messages by specific user.</li>
  </ul></li>

  <li><b>Role Assignment:</b><ul>
    <li>Specify public roles which will be available to anyone using join and leave commands.</li>
    <li>Give the moderators ability to promote or demote people into specified member roles.</li>
  </ul></li>
  
  <li><b>Antispam</b><ul>
    <li>Remove Discord invites.</li>
    <li>Remove mass-mentions.</li>
    <li>Remove literally-spam (duplicate messages)</li>
    <li>Mute people if they type too fast.</li>
    <li>Remove specific links (youtube, twitch, imgur, ...)</li>
    <li>Remove all the links.</li>
    <li>You can also set-up automated ban for spammers. if they continue doing the above.</li>
  </ul></li>
  
  <li><b>Logging:</b><ul>
    <li>Log edited and/or deleted messages.</li>
    <li>Log kicked, banned, and muted users.</li>
    <li>Log people joining or leaving Public Roles, or being promoted/demoted.</li>
    <li>Set up ignored users and channels where the bot will not act or log anything.</li>
  </ul></li>

  <li><b>New Members</b><ul>
    <li>Assign a role to everyone, as soon as they join.</li>
    <li>PM people when they join.</li>
    <li>Set-up welcome / leave messages.</li>
    <li>Verify members with reddit account and assign them a role</li>
  </ul></li>
  
  <li><b>Other Modules:</b><ul>

    <li>Aliases - create an alias from any command, custom or original.</li>
    <li>Polls - simple, custom, and even free types of polls.</li>
    <li>Events - sign-up, check-in, keep track of score.</li>
    <li>Giveaways!</li>
    <li>Meetings - ever heard of the meet-bot for IRC? This is similar.</li>
    <li>Livestream Notifications - set-up a notification for when you go live on Twitch, Hitbox, or Beam.</li>
